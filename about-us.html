Welcome to Riverdale Secondary School!

Here at Riverdale Secondary School, we aim to provide our students with a well deserved education. We give opportunities to all students no matter what. Our supportive staff will dedicate their time to ensuring the students are arriving to our sessions on time, ready to learn something new.

We are a school with high teaching standards, and simple expectations which students should always follow. Join our communications server for more information regarding vacancies or getting to know the Head of Years or Senior Leadership Team.

➟ Sessions begin at: 18:30 and last until 20:15 GMT [Greenwich Mean Time]

❕ If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact a member of the Senior Leadership Team.
