Staff Vacancies

Welcome to the Riverdale Secondary School Staff Team.

The requirements of joining Riverdale staff team are all listed below. Please read them:

- Must be over the age of 13.
- Minimum of 3+ days a week.
- Dedicate your time and effort into Riverdale Secondary School. You are allowed to work at other ro-schools, but the timing there is preferred to be before our sessions.
- You must have experience in professionalism & grammar. People who do not use grammar or maturity, will be striked.

If you have any issues with the application, please speak to: Mr A Wilson, Headteacher.

We wish you the best of luck applying,
